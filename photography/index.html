Hello, My Name is

Jacob Perry

Photographer

Jacob Perry resides in the lush pacific northwest where he enjoys his cold mornings with a warm cup of apple cider. He started taking photographs from a very young age and loved the ability to capture moments in time to document his history on this blue marble floating through space. He is drawn most to photographs of the world around him from mountains veiled is clouds to ocean waves slapping against the rocky shores of the west coast. Though Jacob is not a world traveling photographer he does love to take any chance to go on a hike to enjoy the local beauty around him and inhale the fresh mountain air drenched in petrichor and full of life.
